**Labels:** `enhancement`, `logic-update`, `formatting`
**Assignee:** [Your Name/AI Agent]
**Milestone:** v8.3.1

---

## 1. Context & Problem Statement
**Problem:** The system currently decides arbitrarily where to stop generating bullets (e.g., stopping after Position 3).
**Requirement:** We need a definitive rule set for "How far back to go?" based on the Current Date.
**User Logic:**
1.  **The "Recency" Rule:** Jobs ending within the last 5-6 years MUST be included. (e.g., 2021 end date vs. 2026 current date = Include).
2.  **The "Tenure" Exception:** Older jobs with massive tenure (e.g., 18 years, 2000-2018) represent a "relevant chunk" of history and MUST be included, even if they fall outside the 6-year window.
3.  **The "Ancient History" Fade:** Jobs ending significantly in the past (e.g., >8-10 years ago) with short/standard tenure should only be included if word count/page space permits.
