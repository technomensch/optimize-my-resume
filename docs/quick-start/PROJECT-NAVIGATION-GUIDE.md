## ‚≠ê Pro Tip: One-Time Resume Upload to Your Project

Once you create your Claude Projects, upload your resume as a project file (game-changer!):

**For "Should I Apply?" Project:**
1. Create file: `resume.txt` with your resume content
2. Upload to project knowledge
3. **Result:** After this, just paste job descriptions - the project automatically uses your resume
4. **Saves:** 30 seconds per job check

**For "Resume Analyzer" Project:**
1. Create file: `resume.txt` with your complete resume
2. Upload to project knowledge
3. **Result:** After this, just ask "Analyze my resume" - the project finds and uses it
4. **Saves:** Copy/paste time forever

**Benefits:**
- ‚úÖ Consistent analysis across all checks
